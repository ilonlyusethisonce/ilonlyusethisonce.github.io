3D Modeling Using SketchUp

For our second project, we used Sketchup Free, a free version of Sektchup, a 3D modeling software. We combined this with our previous project, the prosthetics, and after finishing a number of tutorials on basic modeling, we modeled an attachement to a prosthetic hand that would be useful for someone without the use of a hand. I designed an attachement that would be able to hold a water bottle. We then designed a ring with a similar use to the attachement, and printed it.

WhiteBoxLearning Prosthetics

In our first project of the year, we modeled a prosthetic hand using the website WhiteBoxLearning. WhiteBoxLearning is a website which uses a 3D Cloud-based virtual modeling system, which allow users to simulate various projects, such as a prosthetic arm or a rover. We did background research on prosethetics, and then began to work on a prosthetic design and entered competitions. We then transfered our digital designs to models made out of balsa wood. We tested these by launching ping-pong balls and competed with a ping-pong ball launching competition.
